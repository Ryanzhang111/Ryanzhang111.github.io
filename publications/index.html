  3. Mitigating Overestimation in Offline Reinforcement Learning via Inhibiting Error Exacerbation
    Fan Zhang, Wenyu Chen, Mingsheng Fu, Siying Wang, Hong Qu, Zhang Yi.
    IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems (TNNLS) (Under Review).
    Abstract: We prove that not only OOD but also in-distribution state-actions introduce the approximation error, which relates to its sparsity. we contrive a state-sparsity-aware Markov Decision Process to explicit consider the state sparsity in temporal difference (TD) update, inhibiting the error exacerbation.
